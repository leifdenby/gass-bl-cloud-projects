Scientists:  Margreet van Zanten and Peter Duynkerke
Institute: Institute for Marine and Atmospheric research Utrecht (IMAU)
Address: Princetonplein 5, 3584 CC, Utrecht
Email:zanten@phys.uu.nl
Model Type:  3D LES
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
(1)  Computation

Computer used for simulation:  Cray C98
CPU time:                     	~120 hours
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
(2)  Numerical Domain

Dimensions:              	As specified in problem description
Time step for dynamics:  	0.5 sec
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
(3)  Numerical Technique

Numerical method:             Finite-difference
Advection scheme:             Kappa=1/3 - scheme; 2nd order accuracy (Ref. 6b)
Time scheme:                           Leap-frog; 2nd order accuracy
Dynamical equations:          Boussinesq
Lateral boundary conditions:  Periodic
Upper boundary condition:     Stress free, fixed gradient for th_l and q_tot
Translation velocity:             3.4 m/s in x-direction
                                 -4.9 m/s in y-direction
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
(5)  Turbulence Closure Scheme

Turbulence closure:                SGS
Specific turb. closure type:    Deardorff TKE eq. without stab. correction
Variables predicted by  the turbulence closure:        Eddy diffusivities
Closure for dissipation rate:   eps = 0.7*e^1.5/L
Closure for diffusivities:      K_m = 0.12*L*e^0.5
                                K_h = 3*K_m
Length scales:                  L = min(dx,z)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Datasets:

  • setA Initial conditions
  • setB Relevant parameters for entrainment
  • setC Hourly averaged (0-1 LT, 1-2 LT, etc) values of turbulent fluxes (resolved plus subgrid), radiative fluxes and cloud properties
  • setD23.5 Horizontal mean profiles averaged over the "midnight" period of the simulation
  • setD36.5 Horizontal mean profiles averaged over the "noon" period of the simulation
  • setE23.5 Turbulent velocity variances, skewness and fluxes (23-24) average
  • setE36.5 Turbulent velocity variances, skewness and fluxes (36-37) average
  • setF23.5 Resolved turbulent kinetic energy and its budget terms (23-24) average
  • setF36.5 Resolved turbulent kinetic energy and its budget terms (36-37) average
  • setG24 (co-)spectral energy at the 24 LT of vertical velocity and total water content at three heights
  • setG37 (co-)spectral energy at the 37 LT of vertical velocity and total water content at three heights
