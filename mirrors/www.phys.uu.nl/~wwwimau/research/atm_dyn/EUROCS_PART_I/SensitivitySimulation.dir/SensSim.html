With the IMAU/KNMI LES several sensitivity simulations have been performed by M. van Zanten with respect to initial and boundary conditions and forcings. Typically the standard simulation is performed with one (or two) parameters changed (see remark below).

Here you can find timeseries for the following variables:

  • cc cloud cover
  • LWP liquid water path
  • z_base
  • z_top
  • w_max
  • ql_max
If you need more varaibles please contact Peter Duynkerke (P.G.Duynkerke@phys.uu.nl).
The generic name of the files is tmser2.0nn where nn is the number of the simulation:

nn remark
01 standard
02 sea surface temperature -1K
03 sea surface temperature +1K
04 inversion jump in liquid water potential temperature is 9K (instead of 12)
05 inversion jump in total specific humidity is -1g/kg (instead of -3)
06 inversion jump in total specific humidity is -5g/kg (instead of -3)
07 inversion jump in tetal and qt is 9K and -5g/kg, repectively
08 horizontal resolution is dx=dy=25m
09 horizontal resolution is dx=dy=104m
12 subsidence and tendencies x0.5
13 subsidence and tendencies x0.1
