Set I compares the hourly average (2-3) vertical profiles of Q1, Q2, Q3_u and Q3_v,
which are the net effects of convective motions upon the large scale fields.
These profiles are calculated from the domain averaged profiles of Teta, qv, u and v
at hour 2 and 3 and the sum of the hourly averaged (2-3) large scale forcings (subsidence)
and radiation (TetaFor, qvFor, uFor and vFor).

  1. height (in meters) where the following variables locate
  2. Q1 = (<Teta(hour+1)> - <Teta(hour)>)/3600 - TetaFor (K/s)
  3. Q2 = (<qv(hour+1)> - <qv(hour)>)/3600 - qvFor (g/kg/s)
  4. Q3_u = (<u(hour+1)> - <u(hour)>)/3600 - uFor (m/s2)
  5. Q3_v = (<v(hour+1)> - <v(hour)>)/3600 - vFor (m/s2)
