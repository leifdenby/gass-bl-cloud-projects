****************************    MODEL DESCRIPTION    ************************

 Description of the model (Meso-NH)

|Scientist Name: Enrique Sanchez and Joan Cuxart
|Model Type :3D LES
|(1) Computation
|
|Computer used for simulation: CRAY C98
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
|(3) Numerical Technique
|
|Numerical method: finite-difference
|Advection scheme and its order of accuracy: 2nd order centered for U,V,W
| + 2nd order centered + flux correction for Theta + TKE + Water species 
|Time scheme and its order of accuracy: Leap-frog; 2nd order centered
|Dynamical equations : anelastic
|Lateral boundary conditions: Periodic
|Upper boundary condition: Rigid lid with an absorbing layer
|How was translation velocity of the reference frame chosen?: no translation
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
|(4) Physical Parameterizations
|
|Condensation Scheme: all-nothing 
|Microphysical parameterizations: None 
|Drizzle/Rain parameterization: None
|Other comments on physical parameterizations:
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
|(5) Turbulence Closure Scheme (if applicable)
|
|Turbulence closure : SGS  
|Specific turbulence closure type (Mellor-Yamada, etc.): TKE closure, 2 1/2
| level of Mellor-Yamada, diagnostic mixing length equal to grid size
|Variables predicted by the turbulence closure : TKE predicted, L diagnosed
|Closure for dissipation rate: Kolmogorov: eps=0.7 *  e^(3/2)/L
|Closure for turbulent dissipation length: L=(Delta_x*Delta_y*Delta_z)**(1/3)
|Closure for turbulent diffusion length scale: the same 
|Closure for diffusivities:   K_m = C_m * L * e^(1/2)
                              K_h = C_h * L * e^(1/2) * psi_3
                              K_q = C_q * L * e^(1/2) * phi_3 
| with psi_3,phi_3 stability funtions related to Prandtl and Schmidt numbers
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Datasets:

  • setA Initial conditions
  • setB Relevant parameters for entrainment
  • setC Hourly averaged (0-1 LT, 1-2 LT, etc) values of turbulent fluxes (resolved plus subgrid), radiative fluxes and cloud properties
  • setD23.5 Horizontal mean profiles averaged over the "midnight" period of the simulation
  • setD36.5 Horizontal mean profiles averaged over the "noon" period of the simulation
  • setE23.5 Turbulent velocity variances, skewness and fluxes (23-24) average
  • setE36.5 Turbulent velocity variances, skewness and fluxes (36-37) average
  • setF23.5 Resolved turbulent kinetic energy and its budget terms (23-24) average
  • setF36.5 Resolved turbulent kinetic energy and its budget terms (36-37) average
  • set1G24 (co-)spectral energy at the 24 LT of vertical velocity and total water content at three heights
  • set1G37 (co-)spectral energy at the 37 LT of vertical velocity and total water content at three heights
  • setHa1.23.5 Hourly average (23-24) vertical profiles of conditionally sampled fields. Updraft Decomposition.
  • setHa1.36.5 Hourly average (36-37) vertical profiles of conditionally sampled fields. Updraft Decomposition.
  • setHb1.23.5 Hourly average {wx}_dd for w and x=(theta_I,theta_v,q_tot,q_l,u,v). Updraft gridpoints.
  • setHb1.36.5 Hourly average {wx}_dd for w and x=(theta_I,theta_v,q_tot,q_l,u,v). Updraft gridpoints.
  • setHa2.23.5 Hourly average (23-24) vertical profiles of conditionally sampled fields. Downdraft Decomposition.
  • setHa2.36.5 Hourly average (36-37) vertical profiles of conditionally sampled fields. Downdraft Decomposition.
  • setHb2.23.5 Hourly average {wx}_dd for w and x=(theta_I,theta_v,q_tot,q_l,u,v). Downdraft gridpoints.
  • setHb2.36.5 Hourly average {wx}_dd for w and x=(theta_I,theta_v,q_tot,q_l,u,v). Downdraft gridpoints.
  • setH3.23.5 Up/Downdraft Decompositions for w-momentum equations.
  • setH3.36.5 Up/Downdraft Decompositions for w-momentum equations.
  • setI23.5 Hourly average (23-24) vertical profiles of Q1, Q2, Q3_u and Q3_v.
  • setI36.5 Hourly average (36-37) vertical profiles of Q1, Q2, Q3_u and Q3_v.
