Example timeseries from a series of integrations of the Met Office CRM. The solid and dashed lines show results from two slightly different versions of the model. The black lines show integrations using the forcing which has been zeroed above 15km (as for the experimental set up). The red lines show results from integrations with the non-zero forcing above 15km.
The upper row is precipitation, the middle row cloud top height and the bottom row is OLR. The three columns show the three different periods.
