Clarification for the timeseries diagnostics.

Thanks to Tomoe Nasuno for highlighting some slightly confusing instructions for the timeseries diagnostics. I will try and claify this below. They have arisen from a case of "cut and paste" from previous documens.
  • Please send in the timeseries at the freqency at which you do the sampling for the profiles and the budgets. i.e. instantaneous.
    except
    If you calculate your time averaged diagnostics by accumulating at very high frequency in the model (e.g. a few seconds) then please use a more reasonable frequency (every minute or so) and make it clear that this is what you have done in a file ctdesc.modeller
  • Contrary to the instructions in the document. The time value should be the time at which the data is output (not the mid-point as there is no averaging).
