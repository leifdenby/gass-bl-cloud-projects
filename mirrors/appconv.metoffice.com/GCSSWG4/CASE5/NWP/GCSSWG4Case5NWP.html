Running the NWP component of Case 5

  • 48-hour forecasts are to be run for each day within 3 periods of the TOGA COARE experiment.
  • The runs are to initialised from ERA 40.
  • Diagnostics are requested for each gridpoint within 1S-3S and 153E-157E for each timestep.
  • Full instructions for running the NWP component of Case 5 are available in here in postscript or pdf.
  • Contributions are welcome from global and limited area NWP models or climate models. Please contact Martin Willett if you are interested.
