Transitional trade cumulus case from ATEX. Aug. 24-26, 1998, Madrid, Spain

BOMEX Trade cumulus case. July 1997, Seattle, WA, USA

    • Case Coordinator: Pier Siebesma, KNMI, Netherlands
    • Siebesma, A. P., and coauthors, 2003: A large-eddy simulation intercomparison study of shallow cumulus convection. J. Atmos. Sci., 60, 1201-1219.  Full text copy.
    • Case summary and goals, detailed case specifications, and preliminary results.
    • Animation of cloud top heights from a BOMEX trade cumulus simulation on an approximately 25 by 25 km domain using 256x256x72 gridpoints and a 100 m horizontal and 50 m vertical grid spacing, made by Dave Stevens of Lawrence Berkeley Labs. Blue cloudtops are at 600 m, just above the subcloud lifted condensation level, and red corresponds to about 2 km cloud tops. Note range of cloud top heights.
